# WHEN TO USE AN LLM

### Use them for:
  - Generating content
  - Question and answering services
  - ChatBots and AI assistants
  - Diffusion (txt2img, txt23d, txt2vid, etc.)
  - Talk-to-your-data applications
  - Anything that involves communication

### Avoid using them for:
- Latency-sensitive workloads
- Simple projects
- Problems we donâ€™t solve with words but with math or algorithm - forecasting, outlier prediction, calculus, etc.
- Critical evaluations
- High-risk projects
